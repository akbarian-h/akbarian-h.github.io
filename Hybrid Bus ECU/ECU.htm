ECU/CCU

§     June 2011-May 2012

Hybrid Bus Central Control Unit

I have been involved in a team of three member designing the Central/Electrical Control Unit (CCU/ECU) of the first Iranian Hybrid Bus. The ECU controls/manages all aspects of the Electrical and Mechanical units of the Hybrid Bus.

Hybrid Bus is an Electronic Vehicle which reduces fuel consumption up to 30 percent by combination of not only mechanical diesel, but also electrical motor systems.

Iran is the first country in the Middle East which has designed and implemented a Hybrid Bus.

The project costs 7.3 Million USD.

Advisor: Prof. Vahid Esfahanian
