Solar Car

 Aug 2010

I designed and implemented Central Control Unit of a solar car in Tehran University under supervision of Dr. Asaei who is very professional in power electronic and clear energy systems.

 

CCU controls and supervises all of the states of the car and transceiver some data trough the CAN2.0B and wireless modem.

Our solar car is named Persian gazelle2, you may find more information on the net.

( http://pgsc.ut.ac.ir/)

Specifications:

  • DC Bus Voltage: 140V

  • Battery  Capacity: 39Ah

  • Battery Type: Lithium-Ion

  • Solar Cell Type: Silicon, 22%, 3W

  • Number of Cells: 405

  • Charging Time by Solar: 5 Hours

  • Curb Weight: ~150kg

  • Motor: In-wheel BLDC 1.8kW (5kW peak)

  • Max Grade Ability: 25%

  • Max Speed: 120km/h

  • Chassis and Body Material: Carbon Fiber
