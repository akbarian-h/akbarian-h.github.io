Digital Variable Load

June 2011

Design and implementation of a High power Digital Variable Load (the patent is to be submitted on Iranian Patent and Intellectual Property Office).

High current discharging of electrical loads including batteries (Electric Vehicles, Solar car) under very low voltage is a challenge. DVL is a stand alone PID controlled system which controls and tests discharging of power sources with predefined curves. It can carry up to 400A under less than 0.4V-and up to 20KW. It communicates with Labview software via USB port and saves and monitors high resolution Analog parameters in a database.
